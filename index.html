The Problem

When a loved one is hospitalised abroad, the system fails you.

  • ⏳
    Transfers take daysTraditional international transfers weren't built for medical emergencies. Hospitals need payment now — the system makes you wait.
  • 💸
    Fees compound the stressBanks charge transfer fees, exchange rate margins, and intermediary costs on every international payment — right when you can least afford it.
  • 📄
    Bills are impossible to understandMedical billing codes, duplicate charges, and clinical jargon leave families paying for things they don't understand and can't verify.
  • 😰
    All of this during a crisisYou're managing fear, distance, and a broken payment process — simultaneously, in the worst possible moment.

Payment Infrastructure

Built on USDC.
Settled on blockchain.

SoulBridge uses USDC — a digital dollar — as its settlement layer. It's stable, fast, and creates an immutable on-chain record of every transaction. This is the infrastructure that makes instant international healthcare payments possible.

Families who need help covering urgent medical costs can also access flexible financial support through SoulBridge — so an unexpected bill never becomes a crisis.

💲
USDC Settlement
USD Coin · Stablecoin · On-chain
  • Stable value — tracks the U.S. dollar, no volatility during payment
  • Settles in minutes, not business days

  • Broad ecosystem support across global markets
